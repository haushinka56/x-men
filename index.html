Cyclops

Cyclops, also known as Scott Summers, possesses the mutant ability to emit powerful beams of concussive force from his eyes. Due to a physiological anomaly, Cyclops' optic blasts are unleashed as a result of his eyes' inability to contain and control the energy. He must constantly wear a special visor or use ruby quartz lenses to regulate his power. Cyclops' optic blasts can be focused into precise, destructive beams, or unleashed in broad, devastating bursts.
